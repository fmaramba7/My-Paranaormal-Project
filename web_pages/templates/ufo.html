UFO SIGHTING

An unidentified flying object (UFO) is any perceived aerial phenomenon that cannot be immediately identified or explained.

According to the data, more UFO sightings are sighted as light sources or circular object in the sky. Triangular object as wells as Fire Balls were regularly reported. While cone shaped objects were least reported. Many reports show that the phenomenon started out as three light object, yellow in color then transitioning to other shapes/colors.
